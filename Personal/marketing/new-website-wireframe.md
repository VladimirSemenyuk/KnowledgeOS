## Для какой ситуации

### Faster coding has not produced faster delivery

- AI tools are already used across engineering.
- Product intent still requires repeated clarification.
- Review, testing, and release remain bottlenecks.
- Adoption differs between teams and is difficult to scale.
- Leadership cannot connect AI activity to throughput, quality, and business impact.

## Механика программы

```text
01 FIND WHERE TIME IS LOST
Map 3–5 completed features and establish the baseline.

                    ↓

02 ACCELERATE ONE FEATURE FLOW
Change the specifications, agentic execution, quality gates,
roles, and decision points around the largest constraint.

                    ↓

03 PROVE IMPACT
Compare cycle time, throughput, defects, and rework with baseline.

                    ↓

04 DECIDE
Scale, adjust, or stop based on evidence.
```

## От 90-дневной программы к операционной модели

### Three transformation horizons

```text
HORIZON 1 — PROVE               HORIZON 2 — SCALE
Months 1–3                      Months 4–6

Remove the largest constraint   Turn successful practices into
Establish the baseline          repeatable AI-native processes
Accelerate one flow             across Product, Engineering and QA
Make a scale decision           Scale metrics, gates and playbooks

                          ↓

HORIZON 3 — EMBED
Months 7–9

Redesign roles and decision rights
Create an AI enablement and governance cadence
Make the AI-native operating model sustainable
```

Под схемой:

> The 90-day stage produces a decision package, not an obligation to continue. A 6–9 month transformation partnership begins only when the first stage demonstrates value and the company is ready to scale the operating-model changes.

## Возможные изменения

- Agent-ready specifications.
- AI-generated acceptance criteria and edge cases reviewed by Product.
- Controlled agentic delivery within architecture constraints.
- Agentic code review and automated quality and security checks.
- Updated Definition of Done and human decision gates.
- Explicit roles, metrics, and escalation paths.

## Что получает клиент

```text
BASELINE              ACCELERATED FLOW              DECISION PACKAGE

Current cycle time    Tested interventions          Scale / adjust / stop
Throughput             Updated operating model       Evidence and risks
Defects and rework    Metrics and quality gates     Next-stage scope
```

## Как защищается решение

> This is the first stage of a managed transformation, not a test of AI tools.

- Success and exit criteria are agreed after baseline.
- Quality and rework are guardrails, not secondary metrics.
- Interventions are limited to one selected flow before scaling.
- Continuation into the 6–9 month transformation is optional and evidence-based.

## FAQ

### Is 90 days enough for a transformation?

No. Ninety days is enough to establish the baseline, accelerate one feature flow, and make an evidence-based scale / adjust / stop decision. Embedding the operating model typically takes 6–9 months.

### What happens if the program does not prove measurable impact?

The company stops or adjusts the program based on evidence. The first stage is designed to reduce the risk of a broader transformation commitment.

### Who owns implementation?

The client retains an internal executive owner. In Independent Advisory, the client coordinates execution. With Artel Transformation Support, program management and cross-functional coordination can be added.

### How is this different from an AI tool rollout?

The object of change is the full product-to-production flow: intent, specifications, agentic execution, validation, quality, roles, metrics, and decision rights.

### What is required from the client?

An executive mandate, an internal transformation owner, access to baseline data, and one team or feature flow suitable for the first stage.
